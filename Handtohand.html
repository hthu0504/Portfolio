Hand in Hand - Local Charity

What?

    Hand in Hand is a student-led charity club that organizes annual community programs such as the Mid-Autumn Festival, Children’s Day, and Lunar New Year celebrations to support underprivileged children and families. Through cultural events, fundraising, and volunteer collaboration, the club fosters joy, connection, and meaningful social impact.

Why?

    We believe that every child deserves to feel valued, included, and supported—especially during meaningful cultural moments. By bringing together students, communities, and donors, we aim to spread happiness, strengthen social bonds, and build a culture of empathy and generosity.

Our goals

  • Organize inclusive, impactful events that foster a sense of belonging and cultural appreciation.
  • Expand fundraising efforts — building partnerships and mobilizing resources — to raise over $2,000 in cash and in-kind donations.
